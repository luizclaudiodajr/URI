Make a program that reads the name of a salesperson, your fixed salary and the total sales made by him in the month (in cash).
Knowing that this seller gets 15% commission on their sales made, report the total to receive later this month, to two decimal places.

Entrance

The input file contains a text (first name of the seller) and two double precision values (double) with two decimal places, representing the fixed salary of the seller and total amount of sales made by this seller respectively.

Output

Print the total that the employee should receive, as the example provided.
