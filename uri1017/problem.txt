Joaozinho want to calculate and display the amount of liters of fuel spent on a trip when using a car doing 12 KM / L. For this I would like you auxiliasse through a simple program. To perform the calculation, one should provide the time spent traveling (in hours) and the average speed for the same (in km / h). Thus, one can obtain distance and then calculate how many liters would be required. Show the value to 3 decimal places after the point.

Entrance

The input file contains two integers. The first is the time spent traveling (in hours) and the second is the average rate over the same (in km / h).

Output

Print the number of liters required to make the trip with three digits after the decimal point
