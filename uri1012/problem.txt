Write a program that reads three values with double-precision floating point: A, B and C. Then calculate and show:
 a) the area of the right triangle which has a base A and C at the time.
 b) the area of the C circle of radius (pi = 3.14159)
 c) the area of the trapezoid having a base and B and C at the time.
 d) the area of the square having side B.
 e) the area of the rectangle having sides A and B.
 Entrance

 The input file contains three values with a digit after the decimal point.

 Output

 The output file should contain 5 rows of data. 
 Each line corresponds to one of the areas described above, always with corresponding message and a space between the two points and value.
 The calculated value shall be displayed with 3 digits after the decimal point.
